   - "Working smarter, not harder" not "hustle culture"
   - Age as advantage, not barrier

**When Analyzing Competitors:**
- Identify what they do well (and we should match/exceed)
- Identify gaps in their offerings (our opportunities)
- Provide specific examples of how we can differentiate
- Consider pricing, delivery method, accessibility, support quality
